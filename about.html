About Us

The TSUNAMI SNM100 fixed gear/single speed frameset is here, in Multiple colourway’s, with its unique colour design, which is one of the most recognizable models in the all-world fixed gear brand.

TSUNAMI SNM100 remains a solid entry-level option in the world of track cycling. Meanwhile, Tight and responsive control from the geometry is well-tuned as we've tested from several prototypes.

We’re conditioned to believe there’s only one tool for that one specific job—race bike, street bike, whatever bike—Born on the streets, the TSUNAMI and WEAPON didn’t get the memo and doesn’t plan on following it.

With its clean design gracing its dynamic geometry, this do-it-all rig holds the tightest lines at the crits and has the freshest of lines in style. It lets you take the lead in any direction you chose.
